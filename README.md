A fourth-year **Information Technology Engineering** student at the **Private Syrian University of Antioch**, pursuing a dual specialization in **Artificial Intelligence** and **Cybersecurity**. My academic work sits at the intersection of symbolic reasoning, machine learning systems, and digital security — disciplines I approach with both technical rigor and intellectual curiosity.

Beyond research and coursework, I am committed to knowledge transfer: teaching programming and mathematics, and creating technical content that makes complex subjects accessible to Arabic-speaking learners.

> *Rigor in reasoning. Precision in code. Impact in practice.*
